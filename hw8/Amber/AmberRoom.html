The Amber Room

The Amber Room is a social media for artist. Where artist can connect with one another trade ideas, techniques, and artist able to comment and contribute to other artist projects. The website will have profiles and a section for the users portfolio. The Amber Room will cater to all forms of art (music, painting, dancing, etc) to help that struggling artist. To create an account it will require the user to verify there account and return a comfirmation number. The Amber Room will also act as a market place so artist may sell there art and will help people who are looking for a certain type of artist.

In a band but you are looking for a lead singer? Head to The Amber Room!
